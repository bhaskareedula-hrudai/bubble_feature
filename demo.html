Bubble Widget

A self-hosted floating support widget for NCPL Ticketing. Look for the chat button in the bottom-right corner.

Embed in any page with one line:

<script src="https://YOUR-BUBBLE-URL/bubble.js"
        data-api-url="https://your-ncpl-backend.vercel.app"
        data-api-key="your_widget_api_key"
        data-app="HR Portal"></script>
⚠️ Update data-api-url, data-api-key, and data-app to match your deployment before using.
